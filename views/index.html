DevOps

DevOps is the union of Development and Operations teams, processes and products to enable continuous delivery to end users. The contraction of “Dev” and “Ops” refers to the joining of both Developers and System Operators teams with shared processes and practices. The core of utilizing DevOps is performing Automation and Testing at every step of the product lifecycle, from Development to Infrastructure.

Automation

A key part to the DevOps practices is integrating automation within every step of the pipeline. This has created a demand for DevOps Engineers and Automation experts in order to perform these tasks. Whilst newer organizations can embrace DevOps and Automation from when they were conceived, larger organizations require an adoption strategy.
